Welcome to 100SMILES Dental Care

Located at 33 Fantail St. in Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care has proudly served the community for over three decades. Our clinic is dedicated to delivering exceptional dental services grounded in professionalism, integrity, and compassion. With an experienced team and advanced facilities, we offer a comprehensive range of treatments, including preventive care, restorative procedures, orthodontics, implants, and cosmetic dentistry. At 100SMILES Dental Care, we remain committed to upholding the highest standards of clinical excellence and ensuring the long-term oral health of every patient.

Who We Serve

Our primary audience includes:

  • Families with young children (ages 3–12)
  • Teenagers and young adults (orthodontics and cosmetic services)
  • Adults (preventive, restorative, and cosmetic services)
  • Elderly patients (special care for age-related dental needs, denture fittings, and gentle check-ups)
